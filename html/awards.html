Oscar Winner Prediction

Awards Night

Posted by Christian Payne on March, 2023

Award season is in full swing with most of the major awards over with and the Oscars scheduled for the 12th. With this in mind, I wondered how predictive the previous awards actually are for predicting the winnners for the Oscars.

As always, the code written for this project is available on my GitHub here.

The Award for Best Film

For this project I'm going to focus on the award for best film and I'll be using data from a number of film awards including the BAFTAs and Palme d'Or.

I've scraped data for these film competitions and constructed a dataset of their winners and nominees going back to 1960.
