UK Election Forecasting and Prediction

Using machine learning to predict UK election outcomes

Posted by Christian Payne on October, 2021

How do you forecast the outcome of an election?

Maybe we could just predict the total votes won by each party. That is certainly a possibility, however that wouldn't necessarily tell us who would be in government at the end of the election. For example Hillary Clinton received more votes than Donald Trump in the 2016 election, however it was Trump who resided within the White House for next four years. The same is also true here in the UK, although not to the same degree.
